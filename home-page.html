Do You Know?


High and low tides are caused by the moon. The moon’s gravitational pull generates something called the tidal force. The tidal force causes Earth—and its water—to bulge out on the side closest to the moon and the side farthest from the moon. These bulges of water are high tides. That’s your feeling after drinking our products!

The moon’s gravitational pull is the primary tidal force. The moon’s gravity pulls the ocean toward it during hig tides. During low high tides, the Earth itself is pulled slightly toward the moon, creating high tides on the opposite side of the planet. And that’s power of Pilsner Urquell!

Environmental Friendly

In April of 2014, the Two Billionth beer container was returned to the Beer Stores under the Bag It Back program — the highly successful deposit return program for beer containers sold through our stores. Over 207,000 tonnes of emissions avoided as a result of the returns programs equivalent to taking 44,752 cars off Ontario’s roads and highways Since we’ve been in business, we’ve kept approximately 70 billion beer bottles from ending up in Ontario landfill sites. If you were to stretch all those bottles out, it would be about 25 trips to the moon and back.
